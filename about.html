Our story

Dallas Appliance Repair Experts – Your Local Repair Specialists

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable appliance repair to every home in Dallas.

Dallas Appliance Repair Experts was founded with a simple mission: bring professional, same-day appliance repair to our neighbors. With over 12 years of experience, our founder saw the need for a service that combines technical expertise with genuine customer care. We started small, but word of mouth quickly grew our business because we treat every home like our own.

Today, we serve the entire Dallas area with a team of certified technicians who share our commitment to quality. We invest in ongoing training and stock our vans with common parts so we can complete most repairs in a single visit. Every repair is backed by a satisfaction guarantee, because we believe in doing the job right the first time.
